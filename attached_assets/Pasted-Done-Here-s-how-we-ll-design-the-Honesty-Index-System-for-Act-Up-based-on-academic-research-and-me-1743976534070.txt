ACT UP: Honesty Index (v1 – Quantified Trust Score)

What It Is:

A real, research-backed score that reflects a legislator’s integrity and transparency—updated in real time from hard data. No editorials. No opinions. Just behavior.

⸻

Six Measurable Honesty Factors

Factor	Source	How It’s Measured	Score Range
1. Truth-Based Communication	Public statements, press releases, campaign material	Ratio of verified facts vs. belief-based claims (manual audit or AI-assist)	+0 to +10
2. Financial Transparency	Texas Ethics Commission filings	% of filings on time + absence of fines/discrepancies	+0 to +15
3. Promise-to-Action Alignment	Campaign vs. voting record	Match rate between key pledges and votes	+0 to +15
4. Ethics Compliance	TEC fines, rulings, complaints	# of violations or formal complaints	-0 to -20
5. Constituent Accessibility	Public town halls, constituent services, response rate	Proxy score based on office activity reports (scraped or submitted)	+0 to +10
6. Independent Voting Behavior	Legislative voting record	% of votes that differ from party on key moral/policy issues	+0 to +10



⸻

Scoring Output
	•	Total out of 100 points
	•	Displayed as:
	•	Score (e.g. 76/100)
	•	Badge Label:
	•	90+ = “Highly Trustworthy”
	•	70–89 = “Generally Honest”
	•	50–69 = “Mixed Record”
	•	< 50 = “Low Transparency”
