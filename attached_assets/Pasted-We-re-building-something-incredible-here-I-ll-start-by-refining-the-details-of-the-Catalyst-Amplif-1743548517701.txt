🧬 Refining the Super User Roles and Pathways

1. 🎯 The Catalyst: Knowledge Drivers Who Fuel Action

Core Identity: Catalysts are the information hubs of the movement. They seek out, distill, and share critical insights that empower others to take informed action.
Personality: Analytical, curious, and resourceful.

✅ Primary Responsibilities:
	•	Curate and share high-quality, unbiased information on legislation, policy, and government processes.
	•	Translate complex legislative language into actionable insights.
	•	Identify misinformation and flag inaccuracies for correction.
	•	Educate and mentor other users on navigating government systems.

🚀 Pathway to Progression:
	•	Advocate: Shares 3+ verified pieces of information that lead to action.
	•	Influencer: Consistently contributes verified insights and fact-checks within the community.
	•	Super Spreader: Builds a reputation for reliability and accuracy, mentoring others.
	•	Movement Builder: Organizes issue-specific knowledge hubs that empower entire communities.

🎁 Incentives for Catalysts:
	•	Exclusive Data Visualization Tools that showcase legislative trends.
	•	Recognition as “Act Up Verified Expert” after sustained, high-impact contributions.
	•	Invitation to Policy Roundtables where Catalysts advise on content improvements.

⸻

2. 📣 The Amplifier: Social Influencers Who Multiply Impact

Core Identity: Amplifiers spread information and action like wildfire—leveraging their networks to expand Act Up’s influence exponentially.
Personality: Social, connected, and persuasive.

✅ Primary Responsibilities:
	•	Share actionable information across their networks to generate engagement.
	•	Mobilize their communities around key legislative moments.
	•	Organize “Action Circles” where small groups coordinate advocacy efforts.
	•	Recruit and onboard new users, ensuring they engage with relevant issues.

🚀 Pathway to Progression:
	•	Advocate: Invites 5+ people who take at least one action.
	•	Influencer: Sustains high-level engagement by activating their network regularly.
	•	Super Spreader: Builds and leads an Action Circle that amplifies influence.
	•	Movement Builder: Organizes community-driven campaigns that create measurable policy outcomes.

🎁 Incentives for Amplifiers:
	•	Ripple Effect Tracker that shows how far their influence spreads.
	•	Recognition as “Community Mobilizer” with exclusive profile badges.
	•	Access to targeted outreach tools that enhance their ability to activate their network.

⸻

3. 🧠 The Convincer: Persuasive Voices Who Inspire Action

Core Identity: Convincers have the power to inspire belief and action by making civic engagement personal, urgent, and achievable.
Personality: Passionate, charismatic, and persuasive.

✅ Primary Responsibilities:
	•	Craft compelling narratives that translate data into emotional motivation.
	•	Engage in one-on-one conversations to move skeptics toward action.
	•	Build trust within diverse communities by highlighting personal stakes in policy.
	•	Lead “Storytelling for Impact” sessions where users learn to frame their experiences effectively.

🚀 Pathway to Progression:
	•	Advocate: Persuades 3+ people to take action after personalized conversations.
	•	Influencer: Consistently crafts and shares high-impact stories that inspire action.
	•	Super Spreader: Trains others in narrative-based advocacy techniques.
	•	Movement Builder: Develops community-wide narrative frameworks that align public sentiment with meaningful policy change.

🎁 Incentives for Convincers:
	•	Recognition as a “Civic Storyteller” with exclusive access to media and storytelling tools.
	•	Invitation to lead advocacy workshops that equip other users with persuasion techniques.
	•	Impact Visuals that track how their stories contribute to increased civic engagement.
