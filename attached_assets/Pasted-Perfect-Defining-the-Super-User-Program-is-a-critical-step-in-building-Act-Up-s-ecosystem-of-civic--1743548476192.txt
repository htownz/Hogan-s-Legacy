Perfect. Defining the Super User Program is a critical step in building Act Up’s ecosystem of civic super spreaders who will drive the 25% tipping point. This program will identify, cultivate, and reward Connectors, Mavens, and Salespeople who not only engage personally but also inspire others to take meaningful action.

⸻

🎯 Goal:

To create a structured, rewarding pathway that transforms engaged users into super spreaders—amplifying their influence, driving viral growth, and embedding transparency and accountability into civic culture.

⸻

🧬 Super User Program Structure

📊 Four Super User Levels:

Each level reflects a deeper commitment to engagement, education, and influence. As users advance, they unlock exclusive tools, recognition, and influence over platform development.

⸻

Level 1: Advocate (Entry Level)

✅ Role: Explore, learn, and take initial action.
✅ Key Activities:
	•	Following legislation and issues.
	•	Taking small actions (contacting reps, submitting comments).
	•	Sharing information within their networks.
	•	Completing introductory tutorials and action pathways.

🎁 Incentives:
	•	Basic profile badge: “Act Up Advocate”
	•	Access to personalized action plans and updates.
	•	Monthly progress report showing their engagement impact.

⸻

Level 2: Influencer (10% Engagement Goal)

✅ Role: Start influencing others by sharing insights and actions.
✅ Key Activities:
	•	Inviting others to join Act Up.
	•	Sharing action results on social platforms.
	•	Participating in peer challenges to boost engagement.
	•	Completing “Ripple Challenges” that expand their reach.

🎁 Incentives:
	•	New badge: “Act Up Influencer”
	•	Unlock advanced analytics showing their network’s engagement impact.
	•	Early access to beta features and exclusive content.
	•	Recognition on monthly leaderboards.

⸻

Level 3: Super Spreader (R0 > 1 Achieved)

✅ Role: Ignite exponential growth by turning individual actions into waves of influence.
✅ Key Activities:
	•	Recruiting and activating at least 5 new users.
	•	Maintaining consistent engagement across multiple issues.
	•	Leading small action groups or “Action Circles.”
	•	Participating in “Viral Impact Campaigns” that challenge their networks to take action.

🎁 Incentives:
	•	Badge: “Act Up Super Spreader”
	•	Ripple Effect Tracker: Visual display of how their actions influence others.
	•	Invitation to exclusive Super Spreader Councils where they advise on platform features.
	•	Recognition in community impact reports showcasing top influencers.

⸻

Level 4: Movement Builder (Sustained Influence)

✅ Role: Lead communities toward sustained, systemic influence.
✅ Key Activities:
	•	Building and leading advocacy communities around key issues.
	•	Hosting local civic action events with Act Up’s support.
	•	Training new super spreaders and empowering them to build networks.
	•	Helping to design and test new features for Act Up.

🎁 Incentives:
	•	Badge: “Act Up Movement Builder”
	•	Direct influence on platform development decisions.
	•	Exclusive platform testing opportunities for upcoming features.
	•	Spotlighted as “Civic Leaders” in Act Up’s reports and marketing.
	•	Potential for funded local advocacy projects supported by Act Up.

⸻

🏆 Recognition and Rewards Framework

🎁 Milestone Rewards for Super Users:
	•	Impact Certificates: Recognize key milestones with verifiable digital certificates.
	•	Exclusive Content and Tools: Early access to advanced analytics, legislative previews, and AI-powered insights.
	•	Leaderboards and Recognition: Public recognition for top super users through Act Up’s monthly impact reports.

⸻

📊 Ripple Effect Tracker:

A visual dashboard that shows how far a super user’s influence spreads:
	•	Direct Impact: Number of people they’ve invited who take action.
	•	Indirect Impact: Second-level influence from those people inviting others.
	•	Legislative Influence: Track actions that lead to measurable outcomes.

✅ Goal:
To show super users how their influence grows exponentially—reinforcing their belief that their actions are driving real change.

⸻

🔥 Viral Challenges and Peer Engagement Incentives

To accelerate growth and encourage super spreaders, we’ll integrate gamified challenges and peer-driven campaigns:

📢 Invite and Activate Challenges:
	•	“5 for the Future” Challenge: Invite 5 friends to join Act Up and take action.
	•	“Influence Wave” Campaign: Create a chain reaction of actions by recruiting at least 3 people who also take action within 72 hours.

🏆 Peer Influence Incentives:
	•	Unlock Ripple Boosters that give super users extra influence power (e.g., amplified alerts, expanded outreach capabilities).
	•	Earn Community Badges for spreading information within targeted districts or issue areas.

⸻

📚 Training and Mentorship:

To help super spreaders refine their skills and grow their influence, Act Up will offer:
	1.	Super Spreader Bootcamps: Virtual workshops that teach advanced engagement tactics.
	2.	Mentorship Programs: Connect top super users with experienced civic leaders.
	3.	Influence Masterclasses: Equip super spreaders with communication, storytelling, and persuasion skills.

⸻

📈 Tracking and Progression Metrics:

To ensure we’re growing the right kind of super spreaders, we’ll track:
	•	Engagement Consistency: Frequency and diversity of actions taken.
	•	Network Growth: How many new users they bring in and how many remain engaged.
	•	Ripple Effect Influence: The impact of their recruits and subsequent waves of influence.
	•	Retention and Sustained Action: Long-term commitment to civic action.

⸻

🧠 Super User Program Launch Plan:

Phase 1: Identify and Seed Super Spreaders
	•	Launch an invitation-only pilot for early adopters.
	•	Identify natural Connectors, Mavens, and Salespeople within the initial user base.

Phase 2: Build Critical Mass
	•	Roll out viral challenges to rapidly grow the super spreader network.
	•	Recognize and reward high-impact super users.

Phase 3: Formalize and Scale the Program
	•	Launch a public-facing Super User Pathway with clear progression levels.
	•	Build community hubs where super users can collaborate and strategize.

⸻

🔥 Final Outcome:

When the Super User Program hits scale, Act Up will have a self-propagating civic virus that continuously spreads engagement, education, and action—pushing us past the 25% tipping point and permanently shifting the balance of power between government and the people.
