Act Up: Reclaiming Power and Redefining Government for the People

Core Vision:

Act Up is not just a platform—it’s a movement to give people back control over the decisions that shape their lives. Built to educate, engage, and empower, Act Up demystifies the legislative process and arms Texans with the tools they need to influence policy outcomes. Through transparency, accountability, and community action, Act Up aims to redefine expectations of government by embedding civic engagement into everyday life.
